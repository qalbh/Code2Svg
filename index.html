SVG to Image Converter

Code2Svg is a free, browser-based SVG to image converter. Paste or upload SVG code and export it as a PNG, JPG, or WebP image — or download the raw SVG or an animated GIF. You get a live, zoomable preview, exact pixel or scale sizing, a background-color option, auto-cropping, and a recolor tool that detects the fill and stroke colors in your artwork. Everything runs on your device, so nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

How to convert SVG to PNG in 3 steps

  1. Paste your SVG markup into the editor, or upload / drag in an .svg file.
  2. Pick a format (PNG, JPG, or WebP) and set the size, background, or scale you want.
  3. Click Download to save the image, or copy the rendered PNG straight to your clipboard.

Features

  • Export to PNG, JPG, WebP, raw SVG, or animated GIF
  • Exact width/height or scale (@1x–@4x) with an aspect-ratio lock
  • Live preview with zoom, pan, rotate, and flip that carry through to the export
  • Recolor fill/stroke colors and add a solid background before exporting
  • Optimize with SVGO and pretty-print (Format) your SVG
  • Turn your SVG into React, React Native, or a Data URI

Frequently asked questions

Is Code2Svg free to use?

Yes. Code2Svg is completely free, with no account, sign-up, or watermarks. Every feature runs in your browser.

Is my SVG uploaded anywhere? Is it private?

Nothing is uploaded. The conversion happens locally on your device using the canvas and DOMParser APIs, so your SVG code and exported images never leave your browser.

Which image formats can I export to?

You can export to PNG, JPG, and WebP raster images, download the raw SVG, or record animated SVGs as an animated GIF.

Can I resize or recolor the image before exporting?

Yes. Export at an exact pixel width and height or a scale factor, add a background color, auto-crop to the artwork, and remap fill and stroke colors before you download.

Does it work offline?

Conversions run entirely client-side, so once the page has loaded the tool keeps working even on a slow or dropped connection.

About

Code2Svg is a free, client-side toolkit for converting and inspecting SVG artwork. It runs entirely in your browser using the canvas and DOMParser APIs — there is no backend, no account, and no server-side processing. It also offers a companion Image to SVG tracing tool.
