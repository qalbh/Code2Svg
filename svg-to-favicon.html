Favicon Generator

Generate a complete favicon set from an SVG, free and in your browser. Paste or upload your SVG and Code2Svg produces everything a modern site needs: a multi-size favicon.ico, 16/32 px PNGs, a 180 px Apple touch icon, 192/512 px app icons, the crisp source SVG, a site.webmanifest, and an HTML snippet to paste into your <head>.

It all downloads as a single ZIP, ready to drop into your site root. Because the icons are rendered locally, your artwork is never uploaded to a server.

How to generate a favicon in 3 steps

  1. Paste your SVG code into the editor, or upload / drag in an .svg file.
  2. Leave the format on ICO (the favicon format).
  3. Click Favicon package (.zip) to download the complete set, then add the included HTML snippet to your <head>.

Frequently asked questions

What is in the favicon package?

A multi-size favicon.ico, 16/32 px PNG icons, a 180 px Apple touch icon, 192/512 px app icons, the source SVG, a site.webmanifest, and an HTML snippet with the link tags — all in one ZIP.

How do I add the favicon to my site?

Unzip the files into your site root and paste the included head-snippet.html tags into your page’s head. Browsers will pick the right icon automatically.

Do I need a specific image to start?

Any SVG works, but a simple, bold square design reads best at tiny favicon sizes. Logos and icons work far better than detailed artwork.

Is my image uploaded anywhere?

No. Every icon is rendered in your browser and zipped locally — nothing is uploaded to a server.

Is the favicon generator free?

Yes, completely free with no account and no watermark.
