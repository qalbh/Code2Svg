Frequently asked questions

How do I convert a JPG to SVG?

Upload or drag a JPG onto the page, adjust the colors, detail, and smoothing sliders, and copy or download the traced SVG. The tracing runs entirely in your browser.

What kind of JPG converts best to SVG?

Logos, icons, and flat graphics with solid color areas trace cleanly. Photographs and images with gradients or fine detail do not vectorize well — a raster format is better for those.

Can I adjust the traced result?

Yes. The Number of colors, Detail, and Smoothing sliders control how faithfully and how smoothly the image is traced, and the preview updates live.

Does converting JPG to SVG make the image editable?

The trace produces real vector paths you can scale without losing quality and edit in any vector editor, though a traced result will not perfectly match a hand-drawn original.

Is the JPG to SVG converter free and private?

Yes. It is free with no watermark, and your image is traced locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.
