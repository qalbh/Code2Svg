Frequently asked questions

How do I convert a PNG or JPG to SVG?

Upload or drag a PNG or JPG onto the panel, adjust the number of colors, detail, and smoothing, then copy the generated SVG code or download the .svg file. Everything runs in your browser.

Is it free?

Yes. It is completely free and runs entirely on your device. Your image is never uploaded to a server.

Does it work for photographs?

Tracing works best for logos, icons, and flat or simple graphics with solid areas of color. Photographs contain smooth gradients and fine detail that trace into thousands of paths, producing a large, blurry SVG, so they are not a good fit.

Which image formats can I convert?

You can trace PNG and JPG images. Large images are automatically scaled down before tracing so the process stays fast.

What is the difference between a raster image and an SVG?

PNG and JPG are raster formats made of pixels, so they blur when scaled up. SVG is a vector format made of shapes and paths that stays crisp at any size — ideal for logos and icons.

Does it work offline?

Tracing runs entirely client-side, so once the page has loaded it keeps working even without a network connection.
