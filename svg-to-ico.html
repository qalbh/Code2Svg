SVG to ICO Converter

Convert SVG to a real .ico favicon in your browser. Code2Svg renders your SVG to the classic favicon sizes — 16, 32, and 48 px — and packs them into a single multi-resolution .ico file that browsers pick the right size from automatically.

Need more than the .ico? Grab the full favicon package as a ZIP: the .ico, PNG icons, an Apple touch icon, the source SVG, a web app manifest, and an HTML snippet to paste into your <head>. Everything is generated locally.

How to convert SVG to ICO in 3 steps

  1. Paste your SVG code into the editor, or upload / drag in an .svg file.
  2. Set the export format to ICO.
  3. Click Download ICO for the favicon, or Favicon package (.zip) for the complete set.

Frequently asked questions

How do I convert an SVG to ICO?

Paste your SVG or upload an .svg file, set the format to ICO, and click Download ICO. The multi-size .ico is generated in your browser.

What sizes are inside the .ico file?

The .ico packs 16, 32, and 48 px versions, so browsers and Windows can pick the right resolution for the tab, taskbar, or shortcut.

Can I get a full favicon set, not just the .ico?

Yes. The Favicon package (.zip) button also gives you PNG icons, an Apple touch icon, the source SVG, a web app manifest, and a ready-to-paste HTML snippet.

Is my SVG uploaded to a server?

No. The .ico is assembled entirely in your browser — your SVG never leaves your device.

Is the SVG to ICO converter free?

Yes, free with no account and no watermark.
