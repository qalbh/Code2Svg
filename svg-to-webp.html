Frequently asked questions

How do I convert an SVG to WebP?

Paste your SVG or upload an .svg file, set the format to WebP, choose a size and quality, and click Download WebP — it runs entirely in your browser.

Does WebP support transparency?

Yes. WebP keeps the transparent areas of your SVG, so you get PNG-style transparency at a much smaller file size.

Why choose WebP over PNG or JPG?

WebP typically produces smaller files than both PNG and JPG at similar quality while still supporting transparency, which helps pages load faster. It is supported by all modern browsers.

Can I adjust WebP quality and size?

Yes. Use the Quality slider and either a scale preset or an exact custom width and height. The estimated file size updates live.

Is the SVG to WebP converter free and private?

Yes — free, no watermark, and fully client-side. Your SVG never leaves your device.
